About MediSched

Faster and more organized healthcare access for the community.

MediSched is a web-based appointment and healthcare information system created for barangay health centers. It helps reduce long waiting lines, manual appointment processes, and paper-based records.

Patients can book appointments, view clinic schedules, check announcements, and see medicine or vaccine availability before visiting the health center.
